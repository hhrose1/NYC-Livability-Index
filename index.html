Welcome

Created by a transplant who didn't know quite where to look, the New York City Livability Index provides a comprehensive guide to the city. We've tracked neighborhood safety, transit safety, and mapped the city, and will gradually expand this website to make it even more comprehensive with live database connection, StreetEasy-powered rent tracking, and we gradually hope to become the place you come to when you're moving to the big apple.

So welcome to the greatest city in the world: hope you enjoy your stay.
