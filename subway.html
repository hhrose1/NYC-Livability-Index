Understanding the Scores

🔢

Safety Index

This number evaluates both the actual safety and the perception of safety at any given station. Both are heavily influenced by ridership, safety of the surrounding area, isolation, and presence of law enforcement.

⚠️

Crime Index

While the NYC subway system is overwhelmingly safe, some stations have higher rates of crime. This number shows the safety of your chosen station relative to others: a value of 100 means that station is among the highest in terms of crime incidence, not that you have a 100% chance of experiencing a crime.

👁️

Sketchy Index

This number evaluates the perception of safety, calculated primarily by isolation and online reports. Since this combines both nighttime and daytime perception, a station may be perfectly fine during the day but feel less safe at night.
