Neighborhood Overview

The gentrifying western edge of Crown Heights where Franklin Avenue has become a trendy restaurant and bar corridor. Brownstone blocks between Washington and Franklin are increasingly safe and desirable. Ebbets Field Houses NYCHA complex is the main concern at the neighborhood's edge. Noticeably safer and more pleasant than the eastern half, with better dining options and stronger community investment.
