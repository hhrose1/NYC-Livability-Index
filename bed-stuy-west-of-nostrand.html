Neighborhood Overview

The gentrified half of Bed-Stuy with stunning brownstone architecture, an increasingly strong restaurant scene along Fulton Street, and young professional residents. The western blocks nearest Clinton Hill are the safest, with safety decreasing as you move east. Catcalling and street harassment are reported by women, similar to Harlem. The A/C and G trains provide transit options. Worth considering if you find the right block, but requires street-level homework.
