Neighborhood Overview

Tiny, charming brownstone neighborhood between Brooklyn Heights and Carroll Gardens. Safe, walkable, beautiful tree-lined streets with excellent restaurants and boutique shopping along Court and Smith Streets. Among the lowest crime in Brooklyn. Expensive but slightly less so than Brooklyn Heights next door. The F/G at Bergen Street and the 2/3/4/5 at Borough Hall are nearby.
