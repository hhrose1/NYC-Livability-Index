Neighborhood Overview

Dense South Bronx commercial hub around Fordham Road, one of the busiest shopping corridors in the city. High crime across the 46th and 52nd Precincts with persistent robbery and assault issues. The energy is chaotic rather than dangerous in daylight but the area deteriorates significantly at night. Not recommended for residential purposes.
