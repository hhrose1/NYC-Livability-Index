Neighborhood Overview

Safe, quiet suburban Staten Island neighborhoods along the South Shore. Low crime, family-oriented, car-dependent. The Staten Island Railway provides transit but the full commute to Manhattan (SIR plus ferry plus subway) takes well over an hour. No nightlife, limited dining. Safe in the way that any American suburb is safe.
