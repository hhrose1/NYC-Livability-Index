Neighborhood Overview

Brooklyn's Russian and Ukrainian community along the boardwalk, one stop east of Coney Island. Brighton Beach Avenue under the elevated B/Q tracks is lined with Eastern European restaurants, groceries, and shops. The beach and boardwalk are the main draws. Safer than neighboring Coney Island's NYCHA-heavy residential blocks. Affordable and culturally vibrant but a very long commute to Manhattan and limited nightlife beyond the Russian supper clubs.
