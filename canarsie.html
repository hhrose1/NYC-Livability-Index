Neighborhood Overview

Isolated southeastern Brooklyn neighborhood at the L train terminus. Moderate crime — safer than neighboring East New York and Brownsville but still above the Brooklyn average. The L train provides the only subway connection and it's a long ride to Manhattan. Strong Caribbean-American community but limited commercial amenities. Functional but there are better options at similar price points.
