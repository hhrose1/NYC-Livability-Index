Neighborhood Overview

The best Chinese food in America and one of the most culturally vibrant neighborhoods in the city. The commercial core around Main Street is chaotic and extremely dense but not dangerous — more overwhelming than unsafe. The residential blocks east of Main Street toward Kissena Park are quiet and green. The 7 train terminus means you always get a seat for the commute.
