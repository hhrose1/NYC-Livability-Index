Neighborhood Overview

Quiet, diverse residential neighborhood between Borough Park and Flatbush that flies completely under the radar. Bangladeshi, Mexican, and Orthodox Jewish communities coexist along Church Avenue. Very safe with low crime rates comparable to neighboring Midwood. The F/G trains at Fort Hamilton Parkway provide transit. Affordable rents in older walk-up buildings. No nightlife or trendy restaurants but strong grocery access and a genuinely neighborhood-y feel. One of Brooklyn's best-kept secrets for affordable, safe living.
