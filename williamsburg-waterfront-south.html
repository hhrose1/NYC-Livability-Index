Neighborhood Overview

The luxury high-rise waterfront strip along Kent and Wythe Avenues plus the Hasidic community of South Williamsburg below Division Avenue. The waterfront is safe, expensive, and has stunning Manhattan views from Domino Park. The Hasidic blocks south of Division are very safe with constant foot traffic from the religious community. The L train at Bedford Avenue is the main transit option. The gap between the glassy waterfront towers and the neighborhood two blocks inland is stark.
