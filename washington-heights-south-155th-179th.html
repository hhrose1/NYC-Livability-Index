Neighborhood Overview

The busier, denser southern stretch of the Heights dominated by Dominican culture, Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center, and the George Washington Bridge Bus Terminal. The 168th Street station area benefits from hospital foot traffic and institutional presence. West of Broadway is significantly safer than east, where Amsterdam and Wadsworth Avenues have more drug activity. Persistent catcalling is the most common safety complaint from women. The A express provides fast service to Tribeca. Excellent Dominican food and a vibrant street life that's energetic rather than dangerous on the main corridors.
