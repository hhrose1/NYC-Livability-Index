Neighborhood Overview

Increasingly interesting Queens neighborhood on the Brooklyn border receiving Bushwick's gentrification spillover. New cafes and bars appearing alongside the longstanding German, Polish, and Romanian communities. Safer than Bushwick with lower rents. The M and L trains provide transit options. The western blocks nearest Bushwick trend rougher while the eastern blocks toward Middle Village are solidly safe. A dark horse worth watching for affordable apartments with improving social infrastructure.
