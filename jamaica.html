Neighborhood Overview

Major Queens transit hub where the E/J/Z and LIRR converge, surrounded by elevated crime and persistent street-level disorder. The commercial district around Sutphin Boulevard is rough, especially at night. Residential blocks further from the transit hub are calmer but still not recommended as a first NYC apartment.
