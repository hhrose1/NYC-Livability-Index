Neighborhood Overview

Northernmost Bronx neighborhood with historic Irish-American character and a village feel centered on Katonah Avenue. Safer than most of the Bronx but the 47th Precinct covering this area saw the highest total crime increase in the borough in 2025, so the trend is concerning. The 4 train terminus means a very long commute. Safe enough but remote.
