Neighborhood Overview

The less gentrified half of Crown Heights where the safety picture gets complicated. Ebbets Field Houses NYCHA complex is the primary concern. The Hasidic community along Kingston Avenue creates a safe pocket with active community patrols. East of Nostrand toward Utica Avenue crime increases significantly. Block-by-block evaluation is essential — some streets are lovely brownstone blocks, others are not.
