Neighborhood Overview

Two neighborhoods that blend together, with Ditmas Park being the hidden gem. Ditmas Park has gorgeous Victorian houses, tree-lined streets, and a charming commercial strip along Cortelyou Road with cafes and restaurants. Very safe. The broader Flatbush area along Flatbush Avenue is denser and more chaotic with a vibrant Caribbean community. The B/Q trains at Cortelyou Road and Church Avenue provide transit.
