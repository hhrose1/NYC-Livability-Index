Neighborhood Overview

Quiet, diverse working-class Queens neighborhood that feels like Astoria's more affordable, less trendy neighbor. Very safe with strong Filipino and Irish communities. The 7 train provides solid express service to Manhattan. You'll find good food and friendly streets but minimal nightlife. An underrated option for someone who wants a safe, affordable, transit-connected home base.
