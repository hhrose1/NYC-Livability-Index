Neighborhood Overview

One of the most ethnically diverse neighborhoods on Earth with extraordinary food from every continent. The commercial corridors along Broadway and Queens Boulevard are chaotic and dense. Safe during the day due to sheer crowd volume, less comfortable at night on quieter residential blocks. The southern half below Queens Boulevard is calmer than the northern half near Roosevelt Avenue. Affordable with decent transit on the M/R and 7 lines.
