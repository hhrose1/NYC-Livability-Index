Neighborhood Overview

Industrial-meets-residential Queens neighborhood with almost no subway access and a strong car-dependent suburban character. Very safe, very quiet, and home to a lot of warehouse-to-apartment conversions. You'd need a car or a very high tolerance for buses. Safe but isolated.
