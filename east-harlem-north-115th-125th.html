Neighborhood Overview

The stretch anchored by the dreaded 125th and Lex station. Wagner Houses and other NYCHA complexes drive some of the highest violent crime rates in Manhattan. The commercial corridor on 125th is busy but uncomfortable. Gentrification is creeping east from the Second Avenue subway stations at 96th and 116th but hasn't transformed this area yet. Avoid for residential purposes.
