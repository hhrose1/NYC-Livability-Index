Before You Even Consider an Apartment

You MUST check HPD violations. HPD (Housing Preservation and Development) tracks building code violations, including heat/hot water complaints, pest infestations, structural issues, and landlord neglect. These violations reveal the true condition of a building that landlords won't tell you about.
