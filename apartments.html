Before You Even Consider an Apartment

You MUST check HPD violations. HPD (Housing Preservation and Development) tracks building code violations, including heat/hot water complaints, pest infestations, structural issues, and landlord neglect. These violations reveal the true condition of a building that landlords won't tell you about.

Understanding Your Budget

This tool shows neighborhoods within your budget along with typical rent ranges. If your budget falls at the lower end of a neighborhood's range, expect trade-offs: you may need to accept a walk-up apartment, less space, or a harder search process (walking neighborhoods to find postings, working with brokers, waiting for word-of-mouth opportunities rather than relying on StreetEasy).
