Neighborhood Overview

Gorgeous historic brownstone district between Broadway and St. Nicholas Avenue, home to Convent Avenue and the Hamilton Grange. One of Harlem's safest residential pockets with architecture rivaling Park Slope at a fraction of the price. The blocks between Broadway and St. Nicholas are solidly safe. East of St. Nicholas toward the Polo Grounds Towers NYCHA complex the character shifts significantly. The 1 and A/B/C/D trains provide good transit options.
