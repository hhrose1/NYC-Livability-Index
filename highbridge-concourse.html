Neighborhood Overview

South Bronx neighborhood along the Grand Concourse, a stunning Art Deco boulevard that deserves better than its crime statistics. The 44th Precinct has high violent crime with multiple NYCHA complexes. Yankee Stadium brings game-day crowds but doesn't improve daily livability. Not recommended for a first NYC apartment.
