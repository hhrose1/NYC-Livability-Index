Neighborhood Overview

The less gentrified half of Bed-Stuy with beautiful brownstone architecture but higher crime than the western side. Tompkins Houses and Marcy Houses NYCHAcomplexes influence the northern blocks. The further east toward Broadway Junction, the rougher it gets. Some blocks are perfectly fine but requires careful street-level evaluation before committing.
