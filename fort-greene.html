Neighborhood Overview

Culturally vibrant brownstone neighborhood centered on Fort Greene Park and the BAM cultural district. Excellent restaurants along DeKalb Avenue, strong community identity, and good transit options. Very safe in the brownstone core south of the park. The NYCHA complexes (Walt Whitman, Ingersoll) along the northern Myrtle Avenue edge pull the precinct crime stats up but don't reflect the residential experience a few blocks south.
