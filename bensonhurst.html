Neighborhood Overview

Large, safe residential neighborhood with one of Brooklyn's lowest crime rates. Historically Italian-American, now home to Brooklyn's fastest-growing Chinese community along 86th Street and 18th Avenue. Excellent affordable food options. The D/N trains provide subway access. Quiet nightlife but strong grocery and daily needs infrastructure. A practical, safe, affordable choice that nobody writes articles about.
