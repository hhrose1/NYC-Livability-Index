Neighborhood Overview

Home to the largest park in NYC (Pelham Bay Park, three times the size of Central Park) and one of the safest neighborhoods in the Bronx. Suburban feel with single-family homes and a family-oriented community. The 6 train provides subway access but it's a very long local ride to Manhattan. Safe and pleasant but car-dependent for most daily life.
