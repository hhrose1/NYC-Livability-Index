Neighborhood Overview

Hudson Heights is the safest sub-neighborhood in upper Manhattan, perched on a bluff overlooking the Hudson with quiet prewar residential streets and Fort Tryon Park as your backyard. The Cloisters museum, Buunni Coffee, and charming neighborhood restaurants line 181st Street. The A express reaches Chambers Street in about 32-35 minutes. The deep elevator-only stations at 181st and 190th can feel isolating late at night but the streets above are solidly safe.
