Neighborhood Overview

Unassuming residential Queens neighborhood just south of Kew Gardens with affordable apartments and the E/F trains at Van Wyck Boulevard. Safe, quiet, and functional without being exciting. Strong South Asian community with good food options on Hillside Avenue. One of those neighborhoods nobody talks about because there's nothing wrong with it and nothing flashy about it — which is exactly why rents stay reasonable.
