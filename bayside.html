Neighborhood Overview

Affluent northeastern Queens neighborhood that consistently ranks among the safest in the city. Beautiful waterfront, Bell Boulevard's restaurant row, and a strong Korean and Chinese community. The LIRR provides faster Manhattan access than the 7 train. Feels like a nice Westchester suburb that happens to be in Queens. Safe and pleasant but far from the action.
