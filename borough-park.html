Neighborhood Overview

The center of Brooklyn's ultra-Orthodox Jewish community, one of the largest in the world. Extremely low violent crime with constant foot traffic and active community patrols (Shomrim). 13th Avenue is the main commercial strip with kosher restaurants and shops. Very safe at all hours.
