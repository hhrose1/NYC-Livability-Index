Neighborhood Overview

Small transitional neighborhood between Park Slope and Sunset Park, anchored by the landmark Green-Wood Cemetery. Safe, quiet, increasingly gentrified with new restaurants along 4th Avenue. The D/N/R trains at 36th Street provide good transit. Feels like Park Slope's more affordable little sibling without the stroller congestion. The cemetery itself is a stunning 478-acre green space that doubles as a park.
