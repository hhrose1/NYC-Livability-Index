Neighborhood Overview

Persistently one of NYC's highest-crime neighborhoods for decades. Multiple NYCHA complexes, the 73rd Precinct ranks among the top for shootings citywide. Recent 2025 data shows dramatic improvement (homicides down 86%) but the baseline remains challenging. Not recommended for residential purposes.
