Neighborhood Overview

One of the most culturally diverse neighborhoods on the planet with world-class food from every continent along 74th Street and Roosevelt Avenue. The historic garden apartment district north of Roosevelt Avenue (34th-37th Avenues) is beautiful and safe. The critical caveat: Roosevelt Avenue itself from 74th to 90th Street has documented issues with prostitution, gang activity, and street-level disorder, especially at night. Live north of Roosevelt, cross it to reach the subway, and you'll be fine. The 7/E/F/M/R at Jackson Heights-Roosevelt Avenue provide excellent transit.
