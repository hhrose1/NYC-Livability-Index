Neighborhood Overview

High-poverty Caribbean-immigrant neighborhood with crime rates well above the Brooklyn average. The 67th Precinct has persistently elevated violent crime. Limited subway access and few commercial amenities compared to neighboring Flatbush. The housing stock is mostly older and maintenance quality varies widely. Not recommended for a first NYC apartment.
