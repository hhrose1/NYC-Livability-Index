Neighborhood Overview

One of Brooklyn's highest-crime neighborhoods despite beautiful rowhouse housing stock. Multiple large NYCHA complexes, elevated shootings and violent crime. The 75th Precinct has historically been among the most dangerous in the city. Not recommended for residential purposes.
